## 6. Feasibility & Operational Risk Management

Operating a highly automated venture requires proactive planning for platform changes and API constraints.

| Risk Category | Potential Bottleneck | Autonomous AI Team Mitigation Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Platform Anti-Scraping** | Amazon or Google Maps blocking IP addresses, leading to stale data. | **Coder AI** implements rotating residential proxies (e.g., Bright Data, Oxylabs) and alternates user-agent headers to mimic authentic browser environments. |
| **Regulatory Shifts** | Sudden changes in UK/EU import laws or cosmetics compliance rules. | **Sourcing Expert AI** runs weekly automated sweeps of UK Gov and EU Commission gazettes, dynamically updating the compliance vector database. |
| **False-Positive Infringements** | SellerShield sending a C&D letter to an authorized wholesale distributor. | **E-commerce Expert AI** cross-references an automated "Whitelisted Sellers Registry" before triggering any legal outreach. |
| **Billing & Stripe Disputes** | High chargeback rates due to automated transaction disputes. | **Financial Expert AI** flags high-risk transactions before processing, issuing automated, proactive refunds to disputing users. |
