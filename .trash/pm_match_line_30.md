## 5. Comparative Analysis & Strategic Prioritization (Using RICE)

To determine which concept we should build first to hit the 12-month profit goal most efficiently, we evaluate them using the **RICE Prioritization Framework** (Reach, Impact, Confidence, Effort).

* **Reach:** Number of potential customers we can realistically target per month.
* **Impact:** Expected conversion rate and average customer lifetime value (LTV).
* **Confidence:** Certainty of AI team technical execution without human blockages.
* **Effort:** Development hours required for the AI team to deploy the MVP (1 = High Effort, 5 = Low Effort).

$$RICE\ Score = \frac{Reach \times Impact \times Confidence}{Effort}$$
